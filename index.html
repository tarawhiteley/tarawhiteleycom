Tara Whiteley, Digital Specialist
Tara Whiteley, Digital Specialist

Today's consumers begin their shopping experience online. No matter the size of the purchase, from new homes to new shoes, many consumers spend hours online researching brands and products, and many people focus their attention on online reviews of that product.

This is the reality of word-of-mouse advertising today.

To compete, businesses must be online. It's your first impression: don't leave it to a template on a do-it-yourself site. I'm here to help, and I want to get to know you and your business, and create a personalized digital strategy to help you succeed.

A little about me.

Hello and welcome to my virtual real estate on the world wide web!

I have been a digital storyteller in the real estate industry for five years, specifically focusing on social media and website management for homebuilders during that time.

I have a background in journalism and hold a bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of South Carolina. I am currently pursuing my passion by completing a master’s degree program in web design and online communication at the University of Florida and expect to graduate in 2017.

I am an award winning journalist and photographer, and have experience building up the social and digital presence of builders in the Charlotte area.

I would love to help you do the same with your business!
